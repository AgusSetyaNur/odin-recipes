Nasi Goreng Recipes

This food is from indonesia but it has a lot of version in the world

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • oil
  • salt
  • egg
  • garlic

How to make

  1. minced or puree the garlic
  2. heat the oil on the pan
  3. add garlic to the pan fried until golden
  4. add egg
  5. add rice
  6. add the seasoning, sweet sauce, salt
  7. done
