Mie Goreng Recipes

This is a food from asian

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Green mustard
  • Garlic

How to make

  1. Minced the garlic and the vegetable
  2. Heat the oil in the pan
  3. add the garlic to the pan
  4. break the egg and add it to the pan
  5. add noodles
  6. add green mustard
  7. add seasoning, salt and sweet soy sauce
  8. done
