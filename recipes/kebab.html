Kebab Recipes

This is a food from middle east

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Meat
  • Mayonaise
  • lettuce

How to make

  1. grilled the meat
  2. cut the lettuce as you like and put it on top of tortilla
  3. put the meat on top of tortilla
  4. put some mayonaise too
  5. wrap the tortilla and heat it for a while on a pan
  6. done, your kebab ready to serve
